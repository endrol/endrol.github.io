# Juejin Blog Style Guide

## Voice & Tone
- Write like a professional explaining to a smart colleague, not a textbook or a tutorial for beginners
- Use simple, everyday words over jargon — if a simpler word works, use it
- Don't over-explain basics, but don't assume deep expertise either
- Keep it human and direct
